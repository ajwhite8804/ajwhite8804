## How I think about building

A few things that shape how I approach product work:

- **Tech to value, value to tech.** I build in both directions: connecting technology decisions to client and patient value objectives, and translating those value objectives back into the technology. That means keeping UX and UI at the center of every product decision, and getting the right information and capabilities to the right personas, in the right place, at the right time.
- **APIs as products.** A poorly designed API is a broken promise. I treat contracts, versioning, and developer experience with the same rigor I'd apply to any patient-facing feature.
- **Interoperability is a people problem.** The technical standards (FHIR, HL7, SMART on FHIR) are solvable. The hard part is aligning stakeholders across health systems, vendors, and payers who all have competing incentives.
- **Clarity is a feature.** Clear specs, clear decisions, clear tradeoffs. Ambiguity is expensive, especially in healthcare.
